Hytale has launched in early access! This is a slice of our vision for Hytale.

What Is Hytale?

Set out on an adventure built for both creation and play. Hytale blends the freedom of a sandbox with the momentum of an RPG: explore a procedurally generated world full of dungeons, secrets, and a variety of creatures, then shape it block by block.

Encounter dire foes

The world of Hytale is host to creatures large and small, from scuttling critters to powerful monsters. Each zone features its own unique wildlife, with dangerous creatures standing guard over the most powerful gear in the game.

Master the wilderness

Put down roots in any of Hytale's beautiful biomes. Grow your own crops, make your own food, or build a series of minecart tracks to bridge bigger distances and help you explore. You'll have plenty of opportunities to be creative whether you choose to build a cottage or a castle.
