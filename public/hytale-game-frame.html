Hytale has launched in early access! This is a slice of our vision for Hytale.

What Is Hytale?

Set out on an adventure built for both creation and play. Hytale blends the freedom of a sandbox with the momentum of an RPG: explore a procedurally generated world full of dungeons, secrets, and a variety of creatures, then shape it block by block.

Adventure

A WORLD TO EXPLORE

As you traverse each generated landscape, the world will open up around you. Towering ruins, forgotten caverns, and strange landmarks await.

CHOOSE YOUR PATH

Hytale will support many different playstyles. Whether you want to be a settler, adventurer, or craftsperson, you'll have plenty to do.
